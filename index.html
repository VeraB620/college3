Chinese

My mom forced me to take Chinese my freshman year of high school, but I am glad she did. Chinese is an amazing language. The characters are beautiful, and each one has a story, like the character for home is a pig under a roof (thus home is where you can be a pig). Chinese has opened me up to the very cool culture of China. I was even able to go on my schools China trip; it was a huge culture change. I am now a co-president of my schools Chinese Cultural Society. We do activities (like movies, cook dumpling, tea ceremonies, visit China Town) to learn more about the culture. I am glad to help students learn more about the ancient world of China.
I also have recieved 2nd place in the Valparaiso University's Fifth Annual High School Chinese Speech Contest.
